The word count is the number of words in a document or passage of text
Word counting may be needed when a text is required to stay within certain numbers of words
This may particularly be the case in academia legal proceedings journalism and advertising
Word count is commonly used by translators to determine the price of a translation job
Word counts may also be used to calculate measures of readability and to measure typing and reading speeds
When converting character counts to words a measure of five or six characters to a word is generally used for English
